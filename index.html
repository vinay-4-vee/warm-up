About Us

Founded in August 2014, Ghan Marine Products is a trusted name in the seafood export industry, driven by a commitment to quality, sustainability, and excellence. Led by Mr. Karri Krishna Reddy (Managing Director) and Mr. Karri Ajay Bramha Reddy (Executive Director), our company is built on strong values, a deep passion for seafood, and a dedication to delivering premium frozen seafood to markets worldwide.

At Ghan Marine Products, we believe in “Healthy Food and Happy Smiles.” Our journey is deeply intertwined with the trust and support of our valued clients, dedicated employees, and hardworking shrimp farmers. Together, we nurture growth, foster learning, and uphold a legacy of excellence in the seafood industry.

As we continue to expand, our mission remains steadfast—to leave a lasting imprint in the global seafood market while preserving our values for future generations. With every product we export, we carry forward a tradition of quality and a commitment to responsible seafood sourcing, ensuring that our footprint in the vast ocean of seafood excellence endures.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a trusted, successful seafood exporter, importer, and distributor across the worldwide ocean food communities and to provide our customers with high-quality, sustainable sources of shrimp that are a healthy source of protein. We maintain a healthy relationship with not only our clients and employees but also with the seafood farmers who are the building pillars for our quality product.

Our Mission

Our mission is to continuously develop our business model to deliver sustainable and growing value to our clients, shareholders, and employees. We thrive to build an environment where we enjoy coming to work.
